# Naming & icon exploration

Working name is **Sitdown**; the app is being renamed to a Davidson/Wildcat-themed
name. Shortlist, collision checks, and paste-ready icon-generation prompts.

## Name shortlist

| Name | Davidson hook | Fit | Collisions |
|---|---|---|---|
| **HonorRoll** | The Honor Code | "Honor" = trust/on-device privacy; "roll" = roll tape | Cleanest checked |
| **Serval** | Wildcat with the largest ears of any cat — the best listener | Listening app named for the best-listening wildcat | One dormant Android mesh project |
| **Den** | The wildcat's private lair | "The conversation never leaves the den" | Clean but generic dictionary word |
| Chambers | Chambers Building; "in chambers" confidentiality | Privacy resonance | Chambers & Partners (legal) |
| Summit | Summit Coffee on Main Street | "Summit" = a leaders' meeting | Generic; existing apps |
| Norman | Lake Norman | Person-name convention (Otter, Fellow) | Insider-only reference |

Avoid (trademark/collision): Whisker (Litter-Robot), CatChat, Catbird, Purrview
(Microsoft Purview adjacency).

## Icon generation prompts

Davidson palette: crimson `#AC1A2F`, black, cream/white accents. All prompts
follow iOS icon rules: single centered subject, 60px-readable silhouette,
full-bleed square background, no text, no photorealism, no baked-in corner
radius (iOS applies the squircle mask).

### HonorRoll

**1 — Flat minimal glyph (wax-seal record button)**
Minimal flat iOS app icon, a single centered circular wax seal rendered as a bold geometric record button: a solid crimson red (#AC1A2F) disc with scalloped seal edges, a small solid white inner circle at its center like a REC dot. Flat matte cream background filling the entire square edge to edge. Vector style, two to three colors only, thick confident shapes, strong silhouette readable at 60 pixels, generous padding around the mark. No text, no letters, no photorealism, no thin lines, no gradients, no rounded-corner frame or border.

**2 — Gradient depth, modern iOS (ribbon waveform)**
Modern iOS app icon with subtle gradient depth: a single centered honor-ribbon medal whose two ribbon tails morph into a smooth audio waveform at the bottom. Medal disc in rich crimson red (#AC1A2F) with a soft top-light gradient, ribbon and waveform in deep black, small cream highlight accents. Background is a smooth vertical gradient from warm off-white to pale warm gray filling the full square. Soft inner shadows for gentle dimensionality, bold thick forms, silhouette readable at 60 pixels. No text or letters, no photorealism, no thin lines, no baked-in rounded corners.

**3 — Dark-mode variant (laurel around record dot)**
Dark-mode iOS app icon: a single centered glowing crimson red (#AC1A2F) record dot encircled by a bold, simplified laurel wreath of thick geometric leaves in warm cream, symbolizing honor around recording. Background is near-black charcoal with a very subtle radial glow behind the mark, filling the entire square. Flat vector style with a faint luminous edge on the red dot, chunky shapes, high contrast, silhouette instantly readable at 60 pixels, comfortable margin on all sides. No text, no letters, no photorealism, no thin lines, no rounded-corner frame.
